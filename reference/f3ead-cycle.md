# F3EAD SIGINT Cycle

Quick reference for the Find → Fix → Finish → Exploit → Analyze → Disseminate cycle applied to signals intelligence.

---

## FIND
- Detect signal presence using SDR, spectrum analyzer, or handheld radio
- Log frequency, time, strength, and location
- Identify modulation type and behavior pattern
- Compare against established baseline — flag anything new

## FIX
- Determine source location via direction finding (DF)
- Use multiple bearings from different positions
- Confirm source consistency over time
- Minimum 3 bearings in urban terrain (multipath reflections distort single readings)

## FINISH
- Decide on response: avoid, observe, report, or act
- Alert team of threat or opportunity
- Use signal data to trigger defensive or offensive posture

## EXPLOIT
- Reuse adversary signals (net schedules, transmission patterns, timing)
- Gather intelligence for planning
- Observe behavioral changes after adversary detects your presence

## ANALYZE
- Combine logs, DF plots, and team reports
- Identify trends, signal clusters, or command networks
- Build pattern-of-life over time
- Cross-reference with baseline to isolate true anomalies

## DISSEMINATE
- Report findings to team or leadership
- Summarize frequency behavior, locations, and threat posture
- Keep reports concise, clear, and time-stamped

---

## Key Principle
F3EAD is **iterative**, not linear. Every cycle refines the baseline and feeds the next FIND phase.
